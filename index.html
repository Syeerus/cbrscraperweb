Cape Breton Radio Scraper Results

This page contains searchable results obtained by the cbrscraper project that was run on a cron job during the entire month of December 2019. The idea was to gather statistics about how much Christmas music was played on local radio stations in the Cape Breton area. The stations that had their playlists scraped were:

  • New Country 103.5
  • Giant 101.9
  • The Cape 94.9 (apparently rebranded as The Wave now)
  • cjcb am 1270
  • MAX FM 98.3
